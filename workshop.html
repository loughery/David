For new and hesitant writers

How and why to begin writing your own stories.

This workshop is meant for inexperienced writers, journal keepers, memoir beginners, and anyone who has felt the urge to write but did not know how to begin. It emphasizes personal stories, reflective journal writing, and practical ways to turn everyday life into meaningful pages.

Workshop focus

  • Why ordinary lives contain extraordinary material
  • How journaling helps uncover memory, voice, and detail
  • Simple ways to begin without fear of being “a real writer”
  • Turning life moments into short personal narratives
  • Writing for family, community, publication, or personal meaning

Details

Format: Beginner-friendly workshop talk and discussion

Audience: Adults, retirees, local writers, journal writers, and first-time storytellers
